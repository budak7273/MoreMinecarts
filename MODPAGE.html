Adds additional tiered minecarts. All players require the mod in multiplayer.

Likely incompatible with any other mods that change minecart logic. Or, at least, their logic changes probably won't affect these carts.

New Content

The new minecarts have the same acceleration as the base game's, which means they will take longer to reach their (higher) max speed, and longer to slow down. Plan your trips and railways accordingly.
They have an increased interaction radius (2.0 vs 1.3) so you can get in one from slightly further away.

For reference, the speed of the base-game Minecart is 800.
